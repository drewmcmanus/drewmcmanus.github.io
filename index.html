What we do

We help growth-stage teams ship the things that matter — and build the habits to keep doing it.

Velocity acceleration

We embed senior practitioners — product managers, designers, and engineers — alongside your team. Using Lean, Extreme Programming, and User-Centered Design — and AI-assisted coding tools where they accelerate delivery — we find and fix the constraints slowing you down. You ship more, and your team learns the practices that sustain that pace after we leave.

Legacy modernization

The systems that got you to Series B become the anchor that holds you back at Series C. We modernize core applications incrementally — no big-bang rewrites, no months offline, no betting the business on a single risky migration. Measurable progress from week one.

Product strategy + discovery

When the roadmap is contested or priorities aren't landing, we bring C-suite-level perspective to the conversation. Our founders have lived the decisions you're facing. We help you make the hard calls, align stakeholders, and point the team at the right problems — before any code is written.
